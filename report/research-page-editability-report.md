# Research Page Editability Report

## Introduction

- Editable Content:
  - R&D hub cards (title, description)
  - Objectives list items
  - Ph.D. pursuing dataset (department, count, total)
  - Ph.D. holders dataset (department, count, total)
  - Dean details (name, designation, research interest)
  - Quick-link destinations (URLs)
- Character Limits:
  - Title max 30 characters
  - Description 80-120 characters
- Item Limits:
  - Cards max 4-5
  - Objectives max 5-6
  - Links max 6
- Reason:
  - Maintains clarity and balanced layout

---
